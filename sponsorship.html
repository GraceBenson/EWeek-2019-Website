UF EWeek 2019 Sponsorship Information

The What

Engineers’ Week consists of various, individual events hosted over the course of a week in February. Included are descriptions of each of these events ranging from E-Fair, the biggest showcase of engineering disciplines in Florida, to E-Fest, complete with a concert and other fun games geared to the interest of students at UF. All events are free and open to the public.

The Who

Each year, approximately 40 different societies on campus unite to plan and execute this event. Though the event is hosted by the Benton Engineering Council, the event intends on engaging a diverse assortment of groups including general public to elementary school children to students at UF of all majors and interests

The Why

Donators will receive different benefits depending on the donation. These vary from being listed as a sponsor, to having a team member assigned as your liason. We have always pushed for sustainability, even before Engineers’ Week 2012 was the first certified Green Event in University of Florida’s history, All contributions aid us in this mission.

Below are the benefits for the various donation amounts. The benefits of higher tiers of donations include the benefits of previous tiers. For example, donating $250 would give the donator their name listed as a sponsor on programs at events and our website, as well as a quarter-page ad.
